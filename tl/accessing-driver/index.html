Signing In

If a user has an account with a supported provider (e.g. Google), they can click on the “Log in with provider’” button. This process redirects to the provider’s authentication page, and after a successful login, redirects back to DRIVER with the user’s credentials

Administrators may also create username and password pairs for users to log in.

How to Sign In

You’ll need a Google Account before you can sign in to DRIVER. If you already have one, simply click on the “Log in With Google Account” button to proceed to the website.

Certain administrators of DRIVER may also create and assign username and password combinations for others. For users who have their own non-Google login details provided by DRIVER administrators, you may simply enter your username and password, click the “Sign In” button, and proceed to the website.

Attribute Filters

Attribute Filters help the user narrow down their searches depending on the date of the incident, the reporting agency responsible, and the severity of the incident, among others.

They can be found on the topmost bar of the DRIVER website upon accessing either the Record List or Map Interface.

There are two ways to filter incidents on the DRIVER website: through the Record List, or the Map Interface. Unlike the Map Interface, Attribute Filters are used for both the Record List and the Map Interface of the DRIVER website.

Types of Attribute Filters

Text Search

The Text Search filter can be found in the form of a search field on a bar at the top of both the Record List and the Map Interface.

Searching in the text box will narrow down the incidents to ones that include the search term in a field marked as filterable/searchable in the database designer.

Typing multiple words will perform a search for incidents that have both of the words in one or more fields.

Date Filter

The Date Filter can usually be found in between the Text Search Filter and the Option Filter, and narrows down incident data according to a date interval set by the user.

Clicking on the Date Filter launches a clickable interface to choose minimum and maximum dates for display and analysis. The default range of the Date Filter is the previous ninety days.

Option Filter

Option filters are shown in the form of enumeration fields (such as select lists and checkboxes), and are included as attribute filter dropdown buttons.

Examples of option filters include Reporting Agency (concerned in the incident), Main Cause (of the incident), and Severity (of the incident).

Selecting an option applies that filter to the data, and multiple options may be selected for any filter.

Active filters are highlighted in light blue in the interface.

Numeric Filter

Fields containing numbers may be filtered using a minimum/maximum range in the numeric filter.

Official User Manual for DRIVER

To access the main site, go to roadsafety.gov.ph

Record List

The record list interface is simply a tabular view of what data is shown in the map interface. From this view, users can access more details about an incident, or (depending on the type of user), edit incident data.

Accessing Incident Records

To access the record list, click on the “Dashboard” button on the topmost right corner of the website. A dropdown menu will appear, after which you may click the “Record List” button at the bottom of the list.

Viewing Incident Data

Clicking on the “View” button on the right most side of each entry will prompt a popup to appear, which shows users more details of the incident data.

 

 

Using Filters in the Record List

Filters may be used to further narrow down incidents on the record list. Filters can be selected on the header bar on top of the Record List.

 

Users may choose a filter or combination of filters to apply on the Record List.
